How Zavvy works.

From a barcode or a receipt to your basket's total at every Irish supermarket — in five steps, with the scanning running right on your phone.

  1. 1

    Snap a receipt

    Open Zavvy, point your camera at a shopping receipt, and tap. We read every line, the per-item price, the total and the savings on your device — nothing leaves your phone unless you save the receipt to your history.

  2. 2

    Scan each item's barcode

    The receipt knows the item's description and what you paid, but not its EAN — and the EAN is what lets Zavvy match the item against every other shop. Tap the barcode icon beside each line and scan the product. (You can do this in-store while you're packing, or at home from the bag.)

  3. 3

    Build your basket

    Keep the items you'd actually buy. Adjust a quantity, fix a misread line, drop an own-brand item that won't have a rival. The basket is yours — Zavvy never silently drops a missing item, so the totals stay honest.

  4. 4

    See it priced at every shop

    Zavvy lines your basket up against Tesco, Dunnes, SuperValu, Aldi, Lidl, M&S and others — and shows the running total at each, with the cheapest highlighted. Items that aren't stocked at a given shop show "not available here" rather than vanishing quietly.

  5. 5

    Track your spend over time

    Every saved receipt rolls into a spend history — by week, by store, by category. Tap any bar to see exactly which items made it up. Catch the leaks before they catch you.
