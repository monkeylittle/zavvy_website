Common questions.

Is Zavvy free?
The MVP is free. Long-term we may add a small subscription for power features.
How fresh are the prices?
We refresh every product in our catalogue at least once a week, and re-scrape on demand the first time anyone scans an item.
What about own-brand items?
Own-brand items often have store-specific barcodes and won't appear at other stores. Zavvy shows "not available here" rather than hiding the gap.
