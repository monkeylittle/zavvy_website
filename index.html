Why Zavvy, why now?

Grocery bills have climbed hard these past few years, and a weekly shop can cost noticeably more or less depending only on where you buy it. Every euro stretches less than it used to — so paying over the odds for the same items stings more than ever.

Prices never sit still

The same basket costs different amounts at different shops, and those prices move week to week. Whatever was cheapest last month might not be this month — and there's no way to eyeball that.

Shopping around is a chore

Comparing by hand means juggling apps, leaflets and memory, or driving shop to shop. Most of us don't have the time, so we stick to one store and quietly overpay — week after week.

Zavvy does the legwork

Scan a barcode or snap a receipt and Zavvy compares your actual basket across the major Irish supermarkets in seconds — from prices real shoppers share. No trekking, no guesswork: just where your shop is genuinely cheapest.

Never lose a money-off coupon again.

Those vouchers printed at the bottom of your receipt? Zavvy saves them to your phone and shows the barcode right at the till — so the coupon's in your pocket, not crumpled in a drawer past its date.

Snap & save

Point Zavvy at a coupon — or it's picked up automatically when you snap a receipt. It reads the value, the spend it needs and the dates it's valid.

Scan at the till

Open your wallet and Zavvy shows the coupon's barcode full-screen, ready for the checkout scanner — the printed voucher reproduced exactly.

Common questions.

Is Zavvy free?
Yes — Zavvy is free to use.
Where do the prices come from?
From real shopping. Zavvy builds its price picture from the receipts and scans people share across Irish supermarkets — so the more the community scans, the sharper your comparison.
What happens to my receipts?
Receipts are read on your device. The image stays on your phone — nothing is sent to us unless you choose to report a scanning problem — and any card details printed on a receipt are stripped automatically.
What about own-brand items?
Own-brand items usually have store-specific barcodes, so the same product won't always exist at another shop to compare against. You can still track its price history at the store that sells it — so you'll know when it goes up or drops, even when there's no like-for-like rival to line it up beside.

Bronze & Silver — free, earned by scanning.

Every shopper starts as Bronze. Scan your shops and tag the items as you go, and you'll climb to Silver — both free. (Gold, a paid tier, is on the way.)

Bronze — where you start

  • Compare your basket across UK & Irish supermarkets
  • Scan barcodes to check and record prices
  • Save & share money-off coupons to scan at the till
  • Track your trips, prices compared and receipts
  • Your last 2 weeks of receipt history

Silver — for active members

  • Price-drop alerts on items you've scanned — now live
  • Your full receipt history (Bronze sees 2 weeks) — now live
  • Your spend tracked over time — now live
  • The cheapest shop for your basket (coming soon)
  • Early access to new features (coming soon)

Reaching & keeping Silver

Reach Silver by scanning 4 receipts and tagging 10 barcodes from their line items within two weeks. Silver reflects your last two weeks of scanning, so keep scanning to keep it — let it lapse and you'll slip back to Bronze until you're active again.
