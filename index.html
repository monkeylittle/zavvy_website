Common questions.

Is Zavvy free?
Yes — Zavvy is free to use. We may add an optional premium tier later.
Where do the prices come from?
From real shopping. Zavvy builds its price picture from the receipts and scans people share across UK & Ireland supermarkets — so the more the community scans, the sharper your comparison.
What happens to my receipts?
Receipts are read on your device. The image stays on your phone — nothing is sent to us unless you choose to report a scanning problem — and any card details printed on a receipt are stripped automatically.
What about own-brand items?
Own-brand items often have store-specific barcodes and won't appear at other stores. Zavvy shows "not available here" rather than hiding the gap.
