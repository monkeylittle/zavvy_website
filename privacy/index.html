Privacy Policy

Last updated 24 May 2026. Zavvy is operated by MonkeyLittle. Questions: feedback@zavvyapp.com.

The short version

Scanning runs on your device. We collect the minimum needed to compare basket prices and keep your account. We don't sell your data and there are no advertising SDKs. We do use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used so we can improve it — never your receipt contents.

What we collect

  • Account — your email address, via our sign-in provider (Amazon Cognito), including when you use Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Shopping data — the baskets you build, barcodes you scan, line items parsed from receipts, the prices shown, and your savings totals.
  • Receipt images & scan text — receipts and barcodes are read on your device. The image and recognised text leave your device only when you tap "Report a problem" on a scan, which sends that receipt image, the raw recognised text, the parsed result, and basic device/OS info so we can fix scanning accuracy.
  • Approximate location — used on your device to pick the right country/currency and nearby store. It is not stored by us or sent to our servers.
  • Push notifications — if you allow notifications, we store a device push token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging / Apple Push Notification service) so we can send price-drop alerts on items you've scanned. We delete the token when you sign out, and you can turn notifications off in your device settings at any time.
  • Usage analytics — via Google Analytics (Firebase), we collect app-usage events (screens viewed and actions such as scanning a receipt or recording a price) plus basic device/app info (device model, OS, app version, and a random install identifier). This is used to understand how Zavvy is used and improve it, and never includes your receipt contents, the prices you record, or your email.

Payment details on receipts

Receipts can print a card scheme, masked card number, and payment identifiers. Zavvy automatically removes these before anything is stored or transmitted — card data is never retained, even in a problem report.

How we use it

To provide the price comparison and savings features, operate your account, investigate scans you report so the app gets more accurate, and analyse aggregate usage (via Google Analytics) to see which features are used and improve them. We don't use your data for advertising or profiling.

Sharing

We don't sell your data. We use infrastructure providers to run and improve the service on our behalf: Amazon Web Services (hosting, EU/Ireland region) and Google (Google Analytics / Firebase, for usage analytics). We disclose data only if required by law.

Storage & security

Data is stored on AWS in the EU (Ireland), encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Problem reports are kept only as long as needed to diagnose and improve scanning.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data — including your account — by emailing feedback@zavvyapp.com. You can revoke camera, photo, location, or notification permissions in your device settings at any time (scanning needs the camera to work).

Children

Zavvy is not directed at children and is intended for users aged 18 and over.

Changes

We'll update this page if our practices change and revise the date above.
